Redesign the **Doctor Home Screen (Dashboard)** for the LINESETU doctor mobile app.

The screen must be **highly attractive, fast, and extremely responsive**, designed for real clinic usage where the doctor needs quick insights and actions.

---

## 🎯 Core Goal:

👉 Give doctor a **complete overview + quick control** in one screen
👉 Everything important visible in **<3 seconds**

---

## 🧩 SECTION 1: EARNINGS OVERVIEW (TOP PRIORITY)

Top glass card showing:

* Daily Earnings
* Weekly Earnings
* Monthly Earnings

Breakdown:

* Online (App Platform)
* In-Clinic Consultation Fee
* Emergency Fee (Online + In-Clinic)

Include:

* Attractive mini graphs / charts (line or bar)
* Smooth animated transitions
* Toggle: Daily | Weekly | Monthly

---

## ⚡ SECTION 2: QUICK CONTROLS (VERY IMPORTANT)

Glass panel with:

* Toggle Clinic Shift:

  * Morning / Evening / OFF

* Booking Status:

  * ON / OFF toggle

* Quick Buttons:

  * Add Walk-in Patient
  * View Full Queue

---

## 🔴 SECTION 3: LIVE QUEUE PREVIEW

Show current active queue:

Each row:

* Token Number (bold & large)
* Patient Name
* Age
* Type Badge:

  * Walk-in / Online

Top highlight:

* “Currently Consulting Token #X”

Make it:

* Scrollable
* Easy to scan quickly

---

## 📊 SECTION 4: PATIENT STATISTICS

Glass cards showing:

* New Patients:

  * Daily / Weekly / Monthly
* Follow-up Patients:

  * Daily / Weekly / Monthly

Include:

* Small charts or progress bars
* Clear numeric highlights

---

## 🎨 UI DESIGN REQUIREMENTS

* Dark Glassmorphic UI
* Smooth blur + soft glow
* Clean spacing (8pt grid)
* Rounded cards
* High contrast typography

---

## ⚡ UX REQUIREMENTS

* One-hand usability
* Fast interaction (no clutter)
* Large tap targets
* Clear hierarchy:

  1. Earnings
  2. Controls
  3. Queue
  4. Stats

---

## 💎 COMPONENTS

* GlassCard
* EarningsChart
* ToggleSwitch
* QueueRow
* StatCard
* ActionButton

---

## 🚫 AVOID

* Overloaded UI
* Small text
* Confusing layout
* Too many colors

---

## 🎯 FINAL GOAL

Design should feel like:

👉 A premium doctor control dashboard
👉 Real-time clinic command center
👉 Clean, fast, and highly usable

---

Build complete UI with reusable components accordingly.
